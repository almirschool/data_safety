Almir Urdu respects your privacy and gives you control over your data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your personal data associated with the Almir Urdu app.

1. Who Can Request Deletion?

  • Parents: May request removal of their login and notification access data.
  • Students: Academic data is managed by the school administration and can only be deleted or corrected through them.
  • Administrators: Can request deletion of their account by contacting support.

2. How to Request Deletion

To request deletion, please send us an email with your registered details (name, email, and if applicable, student ID) so we can verify your identity.

3. Processing Time

Once we receive your request, we will review it and respond within 7 business days. If approved, your data will be deleted from our systems in accordance with our data retention policy.
